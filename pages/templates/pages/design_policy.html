Design Philosophy

Our website is designed based on following philosophy.

  • Material Design
    Most of idea is referred from Material Design developed by Google.
  • Reusable Components
    Components on our website is designed to be utilized for all over our website.
  • Simple but Rich
    We aim fast user experience, but do not provide poor time.
  • Well-animated
    We offer cool animation as better user experience.

Colours

Colours are selected from traditional colours, especially Japanese traditional ones, referring from "和色大辞典 (Japanese colour unabridged dictionary)" which offer Japanese colours' code. Based on material design principal, we utilize three colours showed below.

Theme colour
"蘇芳 (Suou)" #9e3d3f
Second Theme colour
"Konpeki" #007bbb
Third Theme colour
"Moegi" #aacf53
Main Char colour
"Yohkan" #383c3c

"Suou (蘇芳)" is a black tinge red colour extracted from Suho (蘇芳), a small tall tree of the Fabaceae family. "Konpeki (紺碧)" is a deep and dark blue like the sunny blue sky in midsummer. "Moegi (萌黄)" is a bright yellow-green colour like young leaves sprouting in early spring. "Yohkan (羊羹)" is a reddish black and purple colour. We consider that all colours are can well expressed our website's calm atmosphere, and this is the reason why these colours are selected.
